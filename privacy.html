Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026
MorseLight does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

01 — What we collect

We collect nothing. MorseLight operates entirely offline. No account is required. No data leaves your device.

02 — Data stored on your device

The app stores the following locally, using Android SharedPreferences:

  • Purchase status (which language packs you have unlocked)
  • User preferences (speed, sound, theme settings)
  • Encoding / decoding history (stored only on-device, clearable in Settings)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

03 — In-app purchases

Purchases are processed entirely by Google Play. We do not handle or store any payment information. Google's privacy policy applies to all payment transactions: policies.google.com/privacy

04 — Permissions

  • Camera — used only for optical Morse code reception (flash detection). No images are stored or transmitted.
  • Flashlight (FLASH) — used to transmit Morse code signals via the LED flash.
  • Microphone — used only for audio Morse code reception. No audio is recorded or stored.
  • Vibration — used for haptic Morse output.

05 — Third-party services

MorseLight does not use any analytics, advertising, or crash reporting SDKs. There are no third-party SDKs other than Google Play Billing.

06 — Children

MorseLight does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. The app contains no advertising and no data collection.

07 — Changes to this policy

If we ever add features that require data handling, we will update this page and notify users via the app store listing. The "Last updated" date above will reflect any changes.

08 — Contact

Questions about this policy? Open an issue on our GitHub repository or contact us at the email listed on the Google Play store page.
